Instructions

How to play the game:

  • Start with selecting your difficulty, the number of questions you want and the category of your questions
  • Press the play button
  • After finishing the game write your name in the text field and press the save button to save your high score

How to filter your high scores:

  • Press the "High scores" button. Start with selecting your difficulty, the number of questions and the category of your played game
  • Press the "check high score" button and all the games with the selected settings will show on the high scores list
